How to use these backgrounds

  1. Download the style that fits your video's energy.
  2. In any editor (Canva, Photoshop, Photopea), place your subject cutout in the center. Every background here keeps its busiest detail at the edges for exactly this reason.
  3. Add 3 to 5 words of large, high-contrast text. Check our thumbnail size guide for safe zones.
  4. Before uploading, run your finished thumbnail through the lineup preview against competitors in your niche.
