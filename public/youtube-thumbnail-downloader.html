How to download a YouTube thumbnail

  1. Copy the link of any YouTube video, Short, or embed.
  2. Paste it in the box above. PlayMETRIC grabs every available size automatically.
  3. Click Download on the resolution you want, or copy the direct image URL.

Every YouTube video stores its thumbnail in up to five sizes: 1280x720 (Max HD), 640x480 (SD), 480x360 (High), 320x180 (Medium), and 120x90 (Small). PlayMETRIC checks which sizes actually exist for your video and only shows you the real ones.

Frequently asked questions

Is it legal to download YouTube thumbnails?
Downloading a thumbnail for reference, research, or fair use commentary is generally fine. The image itself belongs to the channel that made it, so do not reupload someone else's thumbnail as your own. Common legit uses: studying competitors, archiving your own videos, and building mood boards.
Why is there no HD (1280x720) option for some videos?
YouTube only generates the maxresdefault HD file when the uploaded video is HD and the thumbnail was rendered at full size. Older or low resolution uploads may top out at 640x480 or 480x360. PlayMETRIC always shows you the best sizes that actually exist.
Can I download thumbnails from YouTube Shorts?
Yes. Paste the Shorts link and it works exactly the same way as a normal video.
Does this work on mobile?
Yes. The whole site is built mobile first. Paste a link, tap Download, and the image saves straight to your phone.
Do you store the thumbnails or my links?
No. Images stream straight from YouTube's servers to you. Nothing is saved on our end.
