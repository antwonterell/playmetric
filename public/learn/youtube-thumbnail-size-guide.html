YouTube Thumbnail Size: The Only Guide You Need

By PlayMETRIC Team  •  Published July 21, 2026  •  Updated July 21, 2026

TL;DR
  • Upload thumbnails at 1280 x 720 pixels, aspect ratio 16:9.
  • Maximum file size: 2MB. Formats: JPG, PNG, GIF (static), or WEBP.
  • Minimum width YouTube accepts is 640 pixels, but always upload 1280x720.
  • Most viewers see your thumbnail at 168 to 360 pixels wide, so design for small.
  • Keep text and faces inside the center, since UI elements like timestamps cover the corners.

The official spec

PropertyRequirement
Recommended resolution1280 x 720 pixels
Minimum width640 pixels
Aspect ratio16:9
Maximum file size2MB
Accepted formatsJPG, PNG, static GIF, WEBP

The sizes YouTube actually stores

When you upload one thumbnail, YouTube generates and stores up to five versions of it on its image servers. These are the files you get when you download a thumbnail:

File nameDimensionsWhere it is used
maxresdefault.jpg1280 x 720Full quality, embeds, large displays
sddefault.jpg640 x 480Standard definition contexts
hqdefault.jpg480 x 360Most common fallback everywhere
mqdefault.jpg320 x 180Mobile lists, small players
default.jpg120 x 90Tiny lists and legacy surfaces

Note that maxresdefault only exists when the source upload was HD. Older or low resolution videos may top out at hqdefault, which is why some videos have no HD thumbnail to download.

Design for 168 pixels, not 1280

On a desktop search results page, your thumbnail renders around 360 pixels wide. In the suggested sidebar it is 168 pixels. On phones it varies between those. Nobody ever sees your 1280 pixel masterpiece at full size in the feed. Practical rules that follow from this:

  • Three to five words of text maximum, at a size readable when the image is 168 pixels wide.
  • One clear focal point (usually a face) instead of a busy scene.
  • Strong contrast between subject and background. Test it: the PlayMETRIC downloader includes a squint test and shows your thumbnail at real sidebar size next to competitors.
  • Avoid the bottom right corner, where the video duration badge sits, and the bottom edge on hover, where the progress bar appears.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best YouTube thumbnail size?
1280 x 720 pixels at 16:9, saved as JPG or PNG under 2MB. This is both YouTube's recommendation and the size stored as maxresdefault.
Is 1920x1080 okay for a thumbnail?
Yes, any 16:9 image at or above 640 pixels wide works and YouTube will scale it. There is no visual benefit over 1280x720 though, and larger files risk hitting the 2MB limit.
What size are YouTube Shorts thumbnails?
Shorts display vertical at 9:16 (1080 x 1920). On most surfaces Shorts show an auto-selected frame; custom Shorts thumbnails have limited support.
