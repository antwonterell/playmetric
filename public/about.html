About Playmetric

Free tools for YouTube creators, built the way tools should be built.

Why this site exists

Most YouTube utility sites feel like they were abandoned in 2014: popup mazes, fake download buttons, watermarks on a public image, and numbers pulled out of thin air. Playmetric was built out of frustration with exactly that.

Every tool here follows three rules:

  • Free means free. No sign up walls, no watermarks, no "premium" version of a basic feature.
  • Honest numbers. The earnings calculator tells you where its RPM data comes from and shows ranges, not fake precision.
  • Fast everywhere. The whole site is a few hundred kilobytes and works great on a phone.

What we build

Playmetric currently offers a YouTube thumbnail downloader with batch ZIP export, a real YouTube layout preview, and an instant thumbnail quality check, plus a YouTube earnings calculator built on published 2026 RPM data by niche and audience geography. More creator tools are on the roadmap.

How the site is supported

Playmetric is supported by advertising. Ads keep every tool free for everyone. We never sell your data and we never charge for the tools themselves. You can read exactly how ads and cookies work on our privacy policy.
